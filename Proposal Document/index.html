Seamless Technology

Intuitive Online Booking Tool – SAVI

Corporate Traveller’s online booking tool, Savi, is
designed in collaboration with our technology partner,
Serko, and provides users with exclusive content
unique to Corporate Traveller, with easy navigation
through one screen for accommodation and travel
bookings that are simple, fast and easy to complete.

Savi’s machine learning and use of AI delivers an
exceptionally user friendly persona-driven experience
for the traveller, booker, and manager by simulating
online leisure booking sites, whilst providing
personalised recommendations at each step of the
booking process.

Savi contains a range of features unique to
Corporate Traveller, including:

Savi Select

Automates the travel request process by streamlining quotes and
interactions between travel arrangers and their travellers.

This eliminates back and forth emails and screenshots to finalise
an itinerary, removing administration burden.

Savi Voice

Gathers user feedback via ‘crowd-sourcing’ techniques, so you
drive the development and innovation pipeline of our products.

Savi Credits

Ensures credits on hold are flagged for usage to appropriate users
without needing to manage credits manually. Never lose credits
due to missed or expired tickets.

AI and Machine Learning

Dramatically reduces booking times from five minutes to 50
seconds. The machine learning draws on the combined volume
of transactions made within FCTG as a group, which collectively
help to develop Savi’s AI memory.

More Choice

Savi has access to more than 420 airlines and over 12 million
room rates worldwide, plus thousands of exclusive flight, hotel
and car options.

Enriched Content from Multiple Sources

Frequent Flyer memberships will drive tailored content in the fares
drawer, personalising inclusions to match the traveller’s status
(e.g., baggage allowance, lounge access and seat selection).

Trading Counts

Our trading account options enable you eliminate the need to pay individual suppliers by streamlining your travel payments to one consolidated supplier - Corporate Traveller

It's also a great option for avoiding credit card fees and merchant fees.

Chargeback

We can arrange for ancilary hotel and car hire expenses, such a room meals, hotel parking and tolls, to be charged back to Corporate Traveller. This way, additional trip costs are consolidated to one supplier and travellers do not need to provide their card details on arrival.

Chargebacks save our customers a lot of time when managing reconciliation, improve travel policy compliance and provide a better experience for travellers.
